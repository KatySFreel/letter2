We are the cheeky girls, you are the cheeky boys. With Friday nights spent indoors, why not warm up with our cozy new cocktail recipe? Designed by our in-house taste testers, the Cheeky Chai is a seasonal riff on the Espresso Martini, packing a warming punch as the rum ignites the natural spices of our Chai Concentrate.
Ingredients
25ml Rum
25ml Chai Concentrate
50ml Oat M*lk
15ml Galliano or vanilla liqueur
Garnish with a star anise

Method
Pour ingredients into an
ice-filled cocktail shaker. Shake for 20 seconds. Strain into a martini glass. Drop in a star anise.
